🔄 Muscles Work in Pairs

💪

Biceps

  • Located at the top of your arm
  • Contracts (gets shorter) when you bend your arm
  • Pulls the lower arm UP
  • Relaxes when arm straightens
🙋‍♂️

Triceps

  • Located under the arm
  • Contracts when you straighten your arm
  • Pulls the lower arm DOWN
  • Relaxes when arm bends
🎯 One muscle pulls one way, another pulls the other way!

🏃 What Happens When You Exercise?

🏋️

Stronger Muscles

  • Body makes new muscle fibers
  • Muscles get bigger and stronger
  • The more you use them, the stronger they get!
🏊

Longer & Stronger

  • Regular exercise = more endurance
  • Heart and breathing muscles strengthen
  • Running, swimming, cycling exercise heart & lungs
💡 Exercise regularly and you'll be able to do it longer!

🔗 Working Together

🦴

Joints

  • Places where bones meet
  • Allow you to move
  • Have fluid inside for smooth movement
🔌

Tendons

  • Tissues that connect muscles to bones
  • Work like strong ropes
  • Help muscles pull bones
🎪 Muscles + Joints + Tendons = Movement Team!
